FAQ

Do I need any experience to join? +
Not at all. Our beginner workshops are designed for complete starters. If you've never held a nail brush before, you're in the right place.
Are the workshops fully online? +
Yes, all our workshops are live and online. You can join from anywhere, all you need is a phone or laptop and a stable internet connection.
What is the difference between with kit and without kit? +
The workshop content is exactly the same for both. The kit option includes all the professional products shipped to your door so you can follow along and practise after the session.
When will I receive my kit? +
Kits are shipped 2–3 days before your workshop date so everything arrives in time for you to practise along live.
What if I miss the live session? +
We will do our best to accommodate you in the next available batch. Reach out to us on WhatsApp and we'll sort it out.
Will I get a certificate? +
Yes. A digital certificate of completion is issued to every student after finishing the workshop.
How many students are in each batch? +
We keep batches small, usually 30-50 students in online batches, so every participant gets personal attention from the educator.
Can I ask questions during the session? +
Absolutely. Every session is fully interactive. Ask questions live, get corrections in real time, and go at a pace that works for you.
Is there support after the workshop? +
Yes. You get direct WhatsApp access to your educator for 7 days after the session for any doubts or practice questions.
How do I register? +
Click Register Now on any course card, or WhatsApp us directly at +91 88791 92901 and we'll guide you through.
